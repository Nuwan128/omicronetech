PowerPoint Controller - Description

What is the PowerPoint Controller?

The PowerPoint Controller is a powerful tool that allows you to control PowerPoint presentations using voice commands. It is designed to make presentations more interactive and hands-free, enabling you to focus on delivering your content without needing to manually navigate slides. With simple voice commands, you can start, navigate, and end presentations effortlessly.

How to Use the PowerPoint Controller

  • Open a Presentation: Say "Open presentation D my_presentation" to open a PowerPoint file named my_presentation.pptx on the D drive.
  • Open Recent Presentation: Say "Open recent presentation" to open the most recently accessed PowerPoint file.
  • Start Presentation: Say "Start presentation" to begin a PowerPoint slideshow (press F5).
  • Navigate Slides:
    • Say "Next slide" to go to the next slide.
    • Say "Previous slide" to go to the previous slide.
    • Say "Go to slide 5" to jump to a specific slide number.
  • End Presentation: Say "End presentation" to minimize the PowerPoint window.

Key Features

  • Hands-Free Control: Navigate slides and control presentations entirely using your voice.
  • Simple Commands: Easy-to-remember voice commands for all presentation actions.
  • Seamless Navigation: Quickly jump to specific slides or move forward/backward.
  • Open Presentations: Open specific or recent PowerPoint files with voice commands.
  • User-Friendly: Designed for presenters of all skill levels.
